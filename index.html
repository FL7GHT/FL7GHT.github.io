our history

The KOY is japanese restaurant founded by none other than japans 2023 miss univers. During a trip to the beautiful province au Quebec in the east coast of Canada miss Ako Kamo fell in love with the small region of Charlevoix. Shortly after her visit the decided to move permenatly to Charlevoix. Soon after moving the lack off authentic japanese cuisine pushed her to open up her first restaurant in Canada. The Koy was born!
